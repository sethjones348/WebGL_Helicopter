Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Camera controls:

  • Click and drag on the image to look around
  • Scroll with touchpad or mouse on image to zoom in and out

Keyboard controls:

  • w - move the helicopter up
  • s - move the helicopter down
  • a - move the helicopter to the left
  • d - move the helicopter to the right
  • f - move the helicopter forward
  • b - move the helicopter backward
  • y, Y - yaw rotation - will cause the above controls to have undesired functionality
  • p, P - blade pitch rotation
  • r, R - increment or decrement blade rotation speed on back and main rotors
