Here are all of the resources I used from THREE.js to make my scene:

All the different shapes and colors that make up my helicopter:
- https://threejs.org/docs/#api/en/materials/MeshLambertMaterial
- https://threejsfundamentals.org/threejs/lessons/threejs-primitives.html

Adding a texture image to my helicopter pad:
- https://stackoverflow.com/questions/48977924/three-js-three-meshlambertmaterial-texture-is-not-a-property-of-this-materi

Skybox:
- https://redstapler.co/create-3d-world-with-three-js-and-skybox-technique/

Skybox texture taken from:
- https://opengameart.org/content/skiingpenguins-skybox-pack

OBJLoader:
- https://threejs.org/docs/#api/en/loaders/ObjectLoader
- https://raw.githubusercontent.com/mrdoob/three.js/master/examples/js/loaders/OBJLoader.js

Midevil Tower taken from:
- https://www.turbosquid.com/3d-model/free/tower

Camera Control:
- https://threejs.org/docs/#examples/en/controls/OrbitControls
- https://raw.githubusercontent.com/mrdoob/three.js/master/examples/js/controls/OrbitControls.js
